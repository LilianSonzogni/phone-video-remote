Quick Start:

  1. Start backend server on your PC
  2. Find your PC's IP address (ipconfig on Windows)
  3. Enter the IP in the format: ws://192.168.1.X:8080
  4. Click Connect
  5. Control your videos!

Note: Your phone and PC must be on the same WiFi network.
